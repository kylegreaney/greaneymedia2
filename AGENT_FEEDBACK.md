- **Why:** Agents want to see your experience, training, and credentials
- **What to add:**

- **Why:** Agents need multiple ways to reach you quickly
- **What to add:**

- **Why:** Agents want to know WHAT you worked on, not just see the images
- **What to add to each project:**
  - Client name/brand (if allowed)

  - Project type (editorial, commercial, campaign, etc.)
  - Date/year
  - Your role (model, designer, director, etc.)

#### 5. **Video Reel/Showreel**

- **What to add:**

## Final Thoughts:

Your portfolio shows talent and versatility. The main gaps are:
1. **Professional credentials** (resume, headshot, stats)
2. **Context** (what projects were, who you worked with)
3. **Quick overview** (reel, comp card)

Agents are busy - they need to understand who you are and what you've done in under 30 seconds. Make it easy for them to say "yes" to representing you.

Focus on making it clear: **WHO you are, WHAT you do, WHERE you've worked, and HOW to contact you.**
